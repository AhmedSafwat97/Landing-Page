    Margherita

    This classic pizza originated in Naples, Italy and is topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. It is a simple but delicious pizza that highlights the quality of the ingredients. Pizza Margherita is a classic and beloved Italian dish that has become a staple in pizzerias all over the world. Its simple yet delicious combination of ingredients - tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves - has won over countless pizza lovers.

    Hawaiian

    The Hawaiian pizza is a divisive but popular pizza that features tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, ham, and pineapple. It's a sweet and salty combination that can be enjoyed by those who love the combination of fruit and meat. Hawaiian pizza is a controversial but popular pizza that is known for its sweet and savory combination of toppings. The pizza features tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, ham, and pineapple, and is often finished with a drizzle of barbecue sauce.

    Pepperoni

    Pepperoni pizza is a favorite in the United States and features tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and slices of spicy pepperoni sausage. It's a classic pizza that is both savory and satisfying. Pepperoni is a type of dry, cured sausage that is made from beef and pork. It is seasoned with a blend of spices, including paprika, garlic, and cayenne pepper, which gives it its signature spicy flavor. When placed on a pizza, the pepperoni cooks in the oven and releases its spicy oils, creating a deliciously savory aroma.

    Meat Lovers

    This pizza is a hearty and indulgent option for meat lovers. It typically features tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and a variety of meats such as pepperoni, sausage, bacon, and sometimes ham or beef. It's a filling and savory pizza that's perfect for those who love a protein-packed meal.
